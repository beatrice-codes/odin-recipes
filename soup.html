Soup Recipe

Description

A delicious, spicy blend packed full of iron and low in fat to boot. It's ready in under half an hour, or can be made in a slow cooker

Ingredients

  • Flatbread or pita
  • 4-5 Falafels
  • Parsley
  • Lime juice
  • Sriracha
  • 75g Natural yogurth
  • Ground cumin
  • 1-2 Garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce by mixing the yogurt, lime juice, garlic and ground cumin
  2. Put the sauce into the fridge
  3. Warm the flatbread/pita in a frying pan (no oil)
  4. Add sauce to the flatbread/pita
  5. Top with the falafels
  6. Add some parsley
  7. Squeeze some lime juice
  8. Add sriracha sauce
  9. Enjoy!
